S.S. Meridian · That same night · Level II

THE CHART ROOM

You reached the gangway with the manifest — and found it already raised, the pier sliding away into fog. Behind you, a steward pressed a folded slip into your hand:

"R is aboard. Chart room. Come alone."

The chart room is empty when you arrive. But R has left the room itself as a message — for someone who solved Stateroom 77.

Two trails run through this room. They meet at the wireless.

THE LANTERN REPLIES

Two flashes from the dark shoreline. Then four. Then one. The stations are lit — the way is clear.

Behind you, the chart room door opens at last. R steps in: the captain herself, Rhea Moran. "The postcard, the suitcase, the safe — I had to know you could carry it. Forty-one families are sailing under names not their own tonight. Because of you, they'll wake up free."

The manifest was never cargo. It was a promise.
